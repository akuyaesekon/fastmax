Pre-Construction

At Fastmax Construction Limited, we believe that careful planning and preparation are key to the success of any construction project. That's why we offer a comprehensive range of preconstruction services to help ensure that your project is completed on time, on budget, and to your exact specifications. Our preconstruction services include, Site analysis: This involves evaluating the site where the construction will take place, including soil testing, topography analysis, and site surveying. Budgeting and estimating: This includes preparing a detailed budget and cost estimate for the project, based on the scope of work and other factors. Value engineering: This involves analyzing the project design to identify areas where costs can be reduced without compromising on quality. Scheduling and planning: This includes developing a detailed project schedule and plan, including milestones, timelines, and deadlines. Constructability reviews: This involves reviewing the project plans and designs to ensure that they are feasible and can be constructed efficiently. Risk management: This includes identifying potential risks and developing strategies to mitigate or manage them. Permitting and regulatory compliance: This involves ensuring that all necessary permits and approvals are obtained and that the project complies with all relevant regulations and codes. Sustainability consulting: This involves evaluating the project design and construction methods to ensure that they are environmentally sustainable. We work closely with our clients to understand their vision for the project and to identify any potential challenges or opportunities for cost savings.
